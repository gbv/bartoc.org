<div class="about__wrapper">
  <section class="about__content">
    <p>
      The <strong>Basic Register of Thesauri, Ontologies &amp; Classifications (BARTOC)</strong>
      is a database of Knowledge Organization Systems and KOS-related registries.
    </p>
    <p>
      The main goal of BARTOC is to list as many Knowledge Organization Systems as possible
      in one place in order to achieve greater visibility, highlight their features, make
      them searchable and comparable, and foster knowledge sharing.
    </p>
    <p>
      BARTOC includes any kind of KOS from any subject area, in any language, any
      publication format, and any form of accessibility. BARTOC’s search interface is
      available in 20 European languages and provides two search options: Basic Search by
      keywords, and Advanced Search by taxonomy terms.
      A <a href="/contact">circle of editors</a> has gathered around BARTOC from all across
      Europe, and BARTOC has been approved by the
      <a href="http://www.isko.org/">International Society for Knowledge Organization (ISKO)</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
      BARTOC was founded by Andreas Ledl as the Basel Register of Thesauri, Ontologies
      &amp; Classifications at
      <a href="https://www.ub.unibas.ch/">Basel University Library, Switzerland</a>.
      In 2020, the database moved to the Verbundzentrale des GBV (VZG), Germany, where it
      was renamed and ported from Drupal to a new
      <a href="/contributing">technical infrastructure</a>.
      The implementation is
      <a href="https://github.com/gbv/bartoc.org">fully available as Open Source</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
      Read more about BARTOC on Wikipedia:
      <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BARTOC">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BARTOC</a>.
    </p>
  </section>
